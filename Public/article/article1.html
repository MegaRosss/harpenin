TIPS FOR NETWORKING

ACTIONABLE TIPS FOR NETWORKING (ONLINE AND OFFLINE)

Networking is a method of meeting people with similar passion,interest or speculation. It could also be a form of interaction with different types of people in which you all work together to achieve a common goal.

Like a popular saying ends with " Together, we can do great things", there's a limit to what you can do alone.

Your network is that circle you form in which you get to do things done easier and faster, they come in when necessary and helps in important places.

Events bring about opportunities for both offline and online networking, and there are chances that you might make lifelong connections too

Attend an event, who knows if the love of your life is in that next event. Go and find happiness you young individual.

We would be sharing a few tips or let's say hacks to help you around your networking both online and offline.

ONLINE NETWORKING.

1.Make sure to always have a good looking profile picture.

2.Get a nice and descriptive bio.

3. Talk about yourself and your interests whenever you get the chance.

4. Always give your opinions in a polite manner.

5. If there are rules made by the admin of the event, adhere to them.

6. Comment on what you find interesting about other people.

7. Do not be afraid to say what you think ( talk the big talk!)
